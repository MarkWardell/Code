Please tune in to "Howlin' the Blues" on 90.1 FM KPFT (www.kpft.org) every Sunday from 2:00-5:00 pm (CST).  We pledge to bring you the best new blues releases from around the world and let you know what's going on in our local music scene. Ticket and CD giveaways have always proven to be popular along with our live in-studio interviews and performances, and we all agree that Baby Girl's comprehensive music calendar is a valuable resource and asset to our community.

If you happen to miss a show, you can always access the program through the KPFT archives. Simply go to the home page www.kpft.org and click on "on demand". Look for "Howlin' the Blues" and you're there.


 

We would like to thank everyone who called in their pledge of support to "Howlin' the Blues" during the Fall Fund Drive.  It is greatly appreciated.
